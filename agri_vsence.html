How It Works

Our AI-powered weather station collects and analyzes hyperlocal environmental data to enable smarter farming decisions.

1

Sensor Deployment

Advanced sensors are installed on the field to measure temperature, humidity, rainfall, wind speed, soil moisture, and solar radiation.

2

Data Collection

All parameters are captured in real-time and transmitted securely to a cloud-based analytics platform via IoT connectivity (LoRaWAN/GSM).

3

AI-Based Analysis

AI algorithms process raw data to detect patterns, forecast weather conditions, and provide actionable agronomic insights.

4

Real-Time Alerts

Farmers receive instant alerts and recommendations via SMS or mobile app for irrigation, disease prevention, and extreme weather events.

5

Decision Dashboard

Users access an interactive dashboard to view real-time and historical trends, generate reports, and plan precision agriculture activities.
